Question: Are coding bootcamps worth it?


For the most part yes.


Coding bootcamps are a good option to learn information of programming fast. While most employers recognize these camps as a good thing, bootcamps are not regionally or nationally accredited. Which means not every bootcamp will teach you as much as you would learn by obtaining a Computer Science degree. In today's job market, most individuals will be able to find a job after attending a bootcamp. Be aware of the up front cost though.
